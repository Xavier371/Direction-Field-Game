Slope Field Game

Enter an equation for y' in terms of x and y
to map the red point through the yellow boundaries.

How it works

Type y' = f(x, y). Each arrow shows the slope f(x, y) at its grid point. The red curve is the solution through the red dot, traced in both directions until it hits the box.

Your goal: pick a function so the curve exits through both highlighted yellow boundaries (any order). Tap Reset for a new start and targets.
